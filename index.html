Resolution, Inches & PPI

Whats the difference?

Inch

The distance measured from top left corner to bottom right, a diagonal line. Its the size of the screen, not to confuse with resolution

e.g. 24 inches = 60.96cm

Resolution

The number of pixel wide and high that make up the

e.g. 1920x1080 is the resolution and 2,073,600 which is the total pixel count when values are multiplied

Pixels Per Inch (PPI)

The amount of pixels packed per inch,

e.g. 400 PPI means 400 pixels for every inch
